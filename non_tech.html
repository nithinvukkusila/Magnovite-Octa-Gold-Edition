TAG TEAM DEBATE

  • In a tag team debate, there are opportunities for every student to participate.
  • The MC organizes a team of students (no more than five) to represent one side of a debatable question.
  • Each team has a set amount of time (3-5 minutes) to present its point of view.
  • The MC reads aloud the issue to be debated and then gives each team the opportunity to discuss their argument.
  • One speaker from a team takes the floor and can speak for no more than one minute. That speaker may "tag" another member of the team to pick up the argument before his or her minute is up.

ROUNDS AND FINAL

  • Multiple preliminary rounds, with each round having a maximum of 12 participants (6 for + 6 against).
  • Best 2 speakers qualify for the finals.
