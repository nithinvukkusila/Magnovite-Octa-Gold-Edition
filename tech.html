Drone Challenge

A growing ecosystems of drone software and hardware vendors are already starting to cater to clients in agriculture, land management, energy, and construction. Drones will soon be boosting crop yields, verifying insurance claims, and assisting in future Hollywood blockbusters. This event is targeted at developing innovative solutions in areas such as: surveying, traffic management, energy, communications, autonomous driving, navigation, Infrastructure inspections, Construction of roadways and forest roads.

IoT Contest

Home and industry automation is the proposed topic. Every team will come up with an innovative prototype or products based on home and industry automaton and present it in round one. The teams will be scored based on their innovativeness, technical approach, flexibility, affordability, ease of use, manufacturability, ease of integration, reliability. All the teams participating would be called up for a group discussion in round two. The topic will be disclosed an hour advance to event conduction.

Autonomous RC Hover Race

A path will be built over the lake to guide a hover boat of specific dimensions with obstacles through the course, an autonomous hover boat built using multiple sensors is supposed to reach from point A to point B avoiding obstacles in between the path.

Electra

Good at technical concepts based on Circuits? Then take this quiz. The quiz has credits which can be won for each correct answer. Problem statements will be provided for which the team has to purchase components for their circuit built to solve the problem statement using the credits they earned in previous round.

Cipherathon

Data being the key for every industry in the future, encrypting it is the most difficult task. The participants need to encrypt given data using new innovative techniques and algorithms developed by them. In round 2, the other teams attempt to decrypt the encrypted data and the team with the best encryption wins.
